School events

Here are some of the school events for this upcoming month:

DAS Battle Tournament

Where: Campus Arena
When: November 12-20
Description: One of the biggest events of the year, Doge's Academy for Superheroes is the host of their own tournament, where students participate in mock battles that are streamed across the world. This tournament is often used as a good way to gain recognition for students, with those that win both gaining prizes and later employment opportunities as nearly all of the top companies in the world use this tournament to as a sort of interview for possible employees. You better bring your A-game if you want a chance of winning!

Surprise Race!

Where: From dormitories to the cafeteria
When: ???
Description: Done on a random night sometime this upcoming month, the Surprise Race is where all the students of each year race from their dormitories to the cafeteria, using their individual powers and talents. No warning, no rules(except for no deaths), just Go Go Go!

Student Clubs Creation and Registration Day

Where: Main Hall
When: 10am-7pm, October 10
Description: The day that students can register for clubs, or create their own if they wish. The existing clubs will have booths in the Main Hall so come and participate if you can!
