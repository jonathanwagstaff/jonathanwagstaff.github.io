The Weapons Club stands as DAS’s center for combat mastery, discipline, and responsible power development. We train students to understand, control, and refine their superpowers and weapon mastery through structured practice, tactical learning, and guided sparring. Whether your strength lies in blades, ranged weaponry, defensive gear, or unconventional abilities and combat tools, our focus remains the same: precision, control, and growth.

More than a training group, the Weapons Club is a community built on trust, resilience, and mutual improvement. Under our leadership, members are challenged to push beyond raw strength, emphasizing technique, composure, and the responsibility that comes with wielding power. Here, skill is earned, control is cultivated, and potential is forged.
