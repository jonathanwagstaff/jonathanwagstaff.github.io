About the Club

The Weapons Club was founded to provide DAS students with a structured environment for developing weapon mastery and superpower abilities safely, effectively, and ethically. Combat powers demand more than talent — they require discipline, restraint, and technical understanding. Our club exists to bridge that gap, offering focused training, tactical education, and practical experience designed to prepare members for real-world challenges.

Guided by a philosophy of growth and responsibility, the club prioritizes control over chaos and mastery over impulse. Members learn not only how to fight, but when and why their abilities should be used. With our emphasis on loyalty, improvement, and protective strength, the Weapons Club continues to cultivate students who value precision, teamwork, and the weight of the weapons they wield.
